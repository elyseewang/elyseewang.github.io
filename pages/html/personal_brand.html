Personal Brand Map



OBJECTIVE


Create a visual representation of your personal brand.


IDEATION


I began with doing free association and creating mind maps of certain values that I wanted to embody through my work.


I then progressed to thinking about what mediums I would like to express these values through, which industries I found most interesting, and the imapct I wanted to make through these industries.


ITERATION


After narrowing down the categories and items within each categories, I began to brainstorm how best to visually arrange them. Always fascinated by the intersection of science and art I decided to arrange them in the structure of an atom.


The color palette was influenced by colors often used to to depict space. The dark blue representing the night sky and deeper solid colors chosen to mimic planets.
