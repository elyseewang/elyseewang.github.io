ABOUT

WHO
I
AM

Hello! My name is Elyse Wang and I am a recent graduate of the University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts. With a B.A. in Media Arts and Practice, I look forward to helping you create works that help us explore and shape the digital landscape.


WHAT
I
DO

A lover of stories since before I could read them myself, I want to discover new, innovative ways to tell compelling narratives.

After pursuing my passion for photography and film, I now find myself learning how to use code to create new ways of visualizing and communicating narratives. Working mainly with HMTL,CSS, JS, Arduino, and Processing I'm hoping to find ways to create content that educates as well as entertains.
